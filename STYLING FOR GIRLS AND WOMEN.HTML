DIFFERENT TYPES OF DRESSES FOR GIRLS AND WOMEN

The fashion industry continues to evolve as we speak. With trends galore being flaunted left and right, it’s important to stay in tune with what’s haute and what’s not.Each individual is entitled to embracing a style that defines who they are as a person and what they feel comfortable in. Fashion is about celebrating unique and distinct looks that scream individuality from miles away.
From jumpsuits and shirt dresses to evening gowns and what not, we’ve covered all the popular fashion themes with style fixes f.or each category as well as 10 types of dresses for any occasion to chose for your wardrobe.If dress to impress is a part of your style mantra, our wardrobe guide is just what you need. Follow these fabulous tips and tricks and you’ll be on your way to making fashion head waves at any event or destination.

  1. 1. CLUB DRESSES

    2. A night out at the club calls for fashion dramatics depicting a world of their own. Sexy, revealing and statement pieces are definitely the way to go. We’re referring to sequin stunners and ruched detailing to slinky bodycon dresses, these looks are sure to set the dance floor on fire.

  2. 2. CASUAL DRESSES

    3. Casual wear is all about simplicity and stylish comfort. Fabrics that are no fuss, soft and breathable are what you should be aiming for. Silk tops, skinny jeans or shirt dresses are fun options you can experiment with this season. Pair it up with some funky accessories and you’ve got a look like no other.

  3. 3. LITTLE BLACK DRESSES

    4. From white and black tie events to cocktail evenings and wedding affairs, the little black dress is the ultimate savior attire when you’ve got nothing to wear on formal occasions. There is an endless array of styles, cuts and designs the classic black dress can be found in today’s time. It’s all about picking out what looks best on your figure and flaunting it with sheer grace.

  4. 4. BACKLESS DRESSES

    5. When you think of a backless dress, the first thoughts that come to mind are sexy sophistication worth glancing over. Usually worn to formal or semi-formal events, there’s nothing hotter than a backless dress. From jeweled pieces to laced up numbers, a sexy backless dress is a must have in every women’s fashion wardrobe. If you’re slim and trim and want to flaunt that fabulous back, this is the pick for you.

  5. 5.BODYCON DRESSES

    6. Bodycon dresses give your silhouette a body hugging appearance that draws attention to female curves. Tight fitted, stretchy and oh so feminine, that’s bodycon dresses for you. Bodycon dresses generally are tighter along the bust region as well as the hips area. Mostly comprised of blended polyester, this fascinating design is a style trend followed by many possessing the renowned hourglass figure. You can grace occasions like cocktail parties, corporate events or evening receptions in this fantastic style.

  6. 6. FORMAL DRESSES

    7. Formal events have a dress code of their own. Comprising of intricate embellishments, delicate lace knee length dresses, elegant satin floor length gowns and subtle hues with minimal cleavage display are what we refer to as appropriate for a formal affair.

  7. 7. LACE DRESSES

    8. The fabulous thing about lace dresses is that they’re able to strike a balance between being too coquettish or leading towards conservative means. From the queen of England to the duchess of Cambridge, lace is a fabric nothing short of royalty. You can mix and match with playful colors to give you a sexy attitude second to none. What’s not to love about lace attire? It’s a timeless textile that never seems to go out of fashion, is fun and sexy too.

  8. 8. LONG SLEEVE DRESSES

    9. Long sleeves can add a statement making detail finishing to any stylish outfit. Sometimes revealing skin isn’t termed necessary for a number of reasons and that’s where long sleeve dresses come into play. It’s a trend that can be carried out in a great number of dress designs. This includes tunic dresses, pencil dresses, asymmetric dresses etc. The choice is all yours. You can have long sleeves that are straight, ruffled or belle in design. Women of all ages can carry them out to fashion perfection.

  9. 9. STRAPLESS DRESSES

    10. Some occasions call for some skin show, exuding a charm of their own. Strapless dresses bare not only your shoulders and arms but your collarbone too. We definitely recommend strapless dresses for women on the more petite side. We’re referring to those whose collar bones or beauty bones call for attention. Be it operas, theatre night or formal events, you can dawn these beauties almost anywhere. A great style tip to remember is to never leave that collar area exposed alone. Liven it up with a stunning necklace accessory for formal appeal or head towards a choker for a more casual look and you’ll be good to go. Women with flat chests and broad collarbones should avoid this style as it highlights areas they should be disguising.

  10. 10. MAXI DRESSES

    11. Maxi dresses are a name given to a fashion style that’s savvy, comfortable and simple throw on chic at the finest level. Maxi dresses are usually categorized as being an informal attire that reaches the floor or ankle region in length. Most maxi dresses are fitted at the bust region and tend to become loose flowing towards the bottom. It’s a fresh summery bohemian vibe, made out of cotton or polyester. You can pick your preference amongst a huge variety of bold patterns, colors and mesmerizing necklines. Dress it up with wedges and the best accessories or tone it down to a more subtle beach look with flip flops and shades.

  11. 11. MIDI DRESSES

    12. Dress season never ever seems to fade away and we’re not complaining. There’s always an occasion on rise for which dolling up is necessary and midi dresses are a staple in such instances. Midi dresses are renowned for grazing the calf region. The stylish design, inspired by the era of the 50’s silhouettes can be seen in a number of hues, fabrics and elements of sparkle. Mostly preferred on those with a taller frame, midi dresses are undoubtedly a dreamy affair on most occasions.

  12. 12. SHORT DRESSES

    13. Perfect for a girl’s night out in the town, travelling or just simple casual wear for summer season, short dresses can be utilized to the max on numerous occasions. Short dresses are all about showcasing those fabulous legs the right way and we’re not complaining in the least. Play around with color, designs and fabric this season and give yourself a fresh new appeal worth a second glance.

  13. 13.VACATION DRESSES

    14. Travelling to any destination requires some well thought out wardrobe essentials that are sure to reflect the vacation spirit. Comfortable, uplifting and full on free spirited clothes are exactly what we’re talking about. The power of dressing should never be underestimated. Whether you’re relaxing on the sunny beaches of the Bahamas or sipping up cocktails on a mountainside retreat, there’s always an excuse to dress the part. Sun dresses, tunics or Bohemian chic inspired long maxi dresses, whatever gets those holiday vibes going is the direction you should be heading in.

  14. 14. SKATER DRESSES

    15. Skater dresses evolved into the fashion scene with an absolute bang. Inspired by girls seen skating in variants of beautifully embellished, sophisticated attire, skater dresses are must have fashion installment worth every investment. Skater dresses adorn the female silhouette in an effortless manner with frill details present below the waistline giving it a seamless fit. Available in vibrant hues, distinct patterns and textures galore, it’s time to collaborate these inspiring connotations with splendid dress accessories. Since the design tends to be rather short with hems present above the thigh level, slim and rectangular figures will best do justice to this kind of attire. You can wear skater dresses to evening events or casual getaways and look flawless from every angle.

  15. 15.SHIFT DRESSES

    16. As can be guessed by their name, shift dresses are less body hugging and hence attract women of all shapes and sizes. They do not possess a defined waistline, making the wearer appear less curvy in appearance. Hem lengths in shift dresses vary from one dress to the other. It’s a style you can wear to work or even semi-formal events.

  16. 16. GEOMETRIC PRINT DRESSES

    17. The fabulous thing about geometric prints in terms of fashion is the striking appeal they manage to create. Whether you term it as bold, attractive or versatile, geometric print styles add splendid vigor to any spring/summer wardrobe. From bold hued squares, triangles, large circles or polka dots, injecting pattern into clothes can create seamless combinations worth drooling over. And the best bit being, they’ll last for years to come.

  17. 17. ANIMAL PRINT DRESSES

    18. Never boring but always considered as awesome, animal print dresses have always been in and out of fashion trends. Making it’s mark in the early 90’s with a dominance like no other, printed coats, leggings, tops and dresses have all been designed keeping the animal theme in mind. From faux fur coats in leopard print to pants in zebra style, animal prints exude a fierce dominance that’s screams in your face fashion at every instance.

  18. 18. FLORAL DRESSES

    19. Floral prints are a staple fashion pattern seen relentlessly every spring and summer season. Be it orchids, roses, daffodils or chrysanthemums, the list of stunning floral array designs to choose from is endless. Since it appeals to the masses, it’s not surprising to witness some of the biggest fashion labels around the world continuing to use this everlasting whimsical theme in their seasonal collections. From mini dresses and skirts to evening gowns and printed pants, retailers can put a flower upon anything and everything fashion related. Floral print dresses are all about embracing the cheer and romance of the changing seasons, not to mention the splendid fun and joy that comes with it. Whether you’re a fan of vibrant hue or soft pastel shades, floral prints leave a subtle softness that celebrates feminism and girlish charm at every perspective. So bring out your outfit and accessories for floral madness worth flaunting every time of the year.
